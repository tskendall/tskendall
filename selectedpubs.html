Frontiers in Artificial Intelligence Research Topic on Computational Sociolinguistics, which I co-edited.

Three volumes in the Publication of American Dialect Society series on Western Speech - edited by Valerie Fridland, Betsy Evans, Alicia Wassink, Lauren Hall-Lew, and me: Speech in the Western States, Volume 1: The Coastal States (2016), Speech in the Western States, Volume 2: The Mountain West (2017), and Speech in the Western States, Volume 3: Understudied Varieties (2020). The volumes feature a series of new studies on vowel patterns around the continental Western U.S. by a range of research groups.

2019 special issue, co-edited with Charlie Farrington, of American Speech featuring studies with CORAAL.

My article commemorating Labov's ground-breaking work on Martha's Vineyard in the early 1960s: 50 years of language study began on Martha's Vineyard in the Martha's Vineyard Times newspaper, August 16, 2011.
